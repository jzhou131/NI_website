Maria Awarded SFPE October 2024 Student Research Grant

Maria was named as a SFPE Foundation October 2024 Student Research Grant recipient

Maria received a student research grant for her project “Drywall Thermal Damage Quantification Using Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) Techniques,” aimed to advance damage assessment in fire forensic science by using ultrasound techniques to quantify calcination.
