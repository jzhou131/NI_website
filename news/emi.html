Dr. Ni to Present Research on Wood Thermal Degradation at EMI 2025

Dr Negar Elhami Khorasani, Dr Thomas Gernay, and Dr Ni are organizing a mini-symposium at EMI 2025, focused on the research advancements in the resilience of built environments to fire hazards.
