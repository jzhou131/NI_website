Dr. Ni Receives Dual Research Funding Awards

Dr. Ni has been recently awarded two grants: one from UMD's Grand Challanges Program and another from the NIJ. Ni's project in the Grand Challenges Grants Program will research the impact of wind on the fire dynamics of mass-timber structures, while the NIJ award will analyze how architectural finishes affect fire patterns.
